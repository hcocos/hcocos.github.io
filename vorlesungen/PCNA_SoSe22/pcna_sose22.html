General Information on the course PCNA


For the successful participation in the course PCNA you need to solve the lab exercises in groups of 4 people!

In every lab exercise session you are asked to demonstrate and explain your configurations, setups and results!

You have to document you configurations and results, since the machines do not persist your configurations!!!
For every lab exercise you have to prepare slides with your configurations and results and need to be able to present these slides in the lecture!!!
Please upload your presentations on the FRIDAY before your presentation until 12 p.m (noon)!
